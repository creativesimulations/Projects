Instructions how to create a player
======================================

1. Attach a "Player" script to the player prefab.
2. Attach a "MovementRigidBody" script to the player prefab.
3. Attach a "PlayerInput" component to the player prefab.
4. Attach an Animator component to the player prefab.
5. Attach an "AnimatorState" script to the player prefab.
6. Attach a "playerInputHandler" component to the player prefab.
7. Attach a capsule trigger collider onto the head of the prefab to detect animals that are in range in order to attack them.
8. Set the serialized fields on the various components as desired.
